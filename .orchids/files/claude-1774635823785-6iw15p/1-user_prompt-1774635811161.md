Create a modern, responsive, and visually engaging website for a civic-tech innovation project titled “Hyper-Local Targeting Engine”.
🌐 Project Overview:
The platform is a geo-fencing based civic awareness system that targets users within specific geographic boundaries (such as government hospitals, colleges, bridges, and infrastructure development sites). When users enter these zones, they receive real-time, location-based notifications or advertisements that explain:

What development work has been completed
Budget utilization and funding sources
Timeline and progress updates
Civic impact and public benefits

The goal is to enhance transparency, public awareness, and citizen engagement using technology.

🎯 Target Audience:

Urban citizens and commuters
Students and young professionals
Government bodies and civic authorities
NGOs and policy researchers


🎨 Design Requirements:

Clean, futuristic UI with smart city / digital India theme
Use colors like blue, white, and saffron accents
Include maps, location pins, and geo-fencing visuals
Smooth animations and scroll transitions
Mobile-first responsive design
